Fishing business has gone a long way in some years ago particularly in Africa.
Fish farming is the principal form of aquaculture, while other methods may fall under mariculture. It involves raising fish commercially in tanks or enclosures, usually for food.

Fishing has been an important part of human culture since hunter-gatherer times, and is one of the few food production activities that have persisted from prehistory into modern age, surviving both the Neolithic Revolution and successive Industrial Revolutions.
However,there is no much to catfish raising than to feed them well,channel an in-let & out-let water, and a proper monitoring.

The types of feed you can use for feeding are;
  • Ordinary Foreign feed
  • Mixing Foreign and Local feed
  • Ordinary Local feed

The steps while feeding:
  1. Feed slowly
  2. Do not pure feed on a spot
  3. Spread feeds
